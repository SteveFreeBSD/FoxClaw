# Baseline Intents

## balanced
- Practical security posture with low operational friction.
- Prioritize high-confidence signals and actionable remediations.

## strict
- Hardened posture for sensitive environments.
- More aggressive controls and stricter permissions/prefs.

## stig
- Compliance-oriented baseline aligned with rigid policy expectations.
- Emphasize auditability, explicit deviations, and controlled exceptions.
